Class: robo:DifferentialVehicle

"A differential vehicle is a mobile robot whose movement is based on two separately driven velocities of the rotational mechanism of both sides of the robot body. It can thus change its direction by varying the relative rate of rotation and hence does not require an additional steering motion. We can find wheeled differential vehicles or caterpillar vehicles."

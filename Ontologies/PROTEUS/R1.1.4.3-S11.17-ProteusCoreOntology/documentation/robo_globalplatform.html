Class: robo:GlobalPlatform

"This composite system groups at least three other ones: - one robot - one control station - one human agent Its definition is: System of systems is a collection of task-oriented or dedicated systems that pool their resources and capabilities together to obtain a new, more complex, 'meta-system' which offers more functionality and performance than simply the sum of the constituent systems. Currently, systems of systems is a critical research discipline for which frames of reference, thought processes, quantitative analysis, tools, and design methods are incomplete. The methodology for defining, abstracting, modelling, and analysing system of systems problems is typically referred to as system of systems engineering. A “robotic system of systems” is a specialised system of systems where there is at least one “robot”, one “control station” and one “human agent”." [lang: en]

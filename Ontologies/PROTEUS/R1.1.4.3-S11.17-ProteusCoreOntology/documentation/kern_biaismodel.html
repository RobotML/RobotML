Class: kern:BiaisModel

"Evolution Model that could be simulated by an algorithm that introduces a statistical distortion of the acquisition of the input data of the evolution process. This is a distortion because it is systematically different from the population parameter of interest. It can be caused by the selection process of the inputs or how these inputs were obtained. Thus, the inputs of the different states would be different from a non-distortion scenario, so the evolution would correspond to a stochastic model." [lang: en]

Class: expe:Scenario

"A scenario defines operational missions and their operational contexts. It is constituded mainly of documents and derived of the expertise of those working in the field or at least of the model those trying to define it create from their partiala knowledge. A scenario is a generic thing and in the scope of EUropean military vocabulary, in order to obtain a refined description, missions' vignettes are necessary. It is not necessary in the scope of this ontology to refine what is a scenario." [lang: en]

Class: simu:PhysicEngine

"A physics engine is a computer software that provides an approximate simulation of certain physical systems, such as rigid body dynamics (including collision detection), soft body dynamics, and fluid dynamics, of use in the domains of computer graphics, video games and film. The term is sometimes used more generally to describe any software system for simulating physical phenomena, such as high-performance scientific simulation." [lang: en]
